# Молитва Благодарения — Коптская версия

**NHC VI.7.63.33-65.7 — The Prayer of Thanksgiving**

*Кодекс Наг-Хаммади VI. Английский перевод: P.A. Dirkse & J. Brashler, in Nag Hammadi Codices V, 2-5 and VI, with Papyrus Berolinensis 8502, 1 and 4. NHSt 11. Leiden: Brill, 1979, pp. 375-87.*

---

We give thanks to you! Every soul and heart is lifted up to you,
O undisturbed Name, honored with the name 'God'
and praised with the name 'Father'.

For to everyone and everything comes the fatherly kindness
and affection and love, and any teaching there may be that is sweet and plain,
giving us mind, speech, and knowledge:
mind, so that we may understand you;
speech, so that we may expound you;
knowledge, so that we may know you.

We rejoice, having been illuminated by your knowledge.
We rejoice because you have shown us yourself.
We rejoice because while we were in the body
you have made us divine through your knowledge.

The thanksgiving of the man who attains to you is one thing:
that we know you.
We have known you, O intellectual light.
O life of life, we have known you.
O womb of every creature, we have known you.
O womb pregnant with the nature of the Father, we have known you.
O eternal permanence of the begetting Father, we have known you.

Having worshipped your goodness, we ask one favor:
that we be preserved in knowledge.
There is one protection that we desire:
that we not stumble in this kind of life.
